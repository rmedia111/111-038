How to Make Homemade Bread

Making homemade bread is a rewarding process that results in delicious, fresh-baked loaves. Start by proofing active dry yeast in warm water with a pinch of sugar until foamy. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and knead until smooth and elastic. Let the dough rise in a warm place until doubled in size, then punch it down and shape it into loaves. Place the loaves in greased loaf pans and let rise again until doubled. Bake in a preheated oven at 375Â°F for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped on the bottom.
